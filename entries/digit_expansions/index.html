Digit Expansions

Jonas Bayer, Marco David, Abhik Pal and Benedikt Stock

20 April 2022

This is an unofficial reimagining of the Archive of Formal Proofs

Abstract

We formalize how a natural number can be expanded into its digits in some base and prove properties about functions that operate on digit expansions. This includes the formalization of concepts such as digit shifts and carries. For a base that is a power of 2 we formalize the binary AND, binary orthogonality and binary masking of two natural numbers. This library on digit expansions builds the basis for the formalization of the DPRM theorem.

No Faster-Than-Light Observers

Mike Stannett and István Németi

28 April 2016

Abstract

We provide a formal proof within First Order Relativity Theory that no observer can travel faster than the speed of light. Originally reported in Stannett & Németi (2014) "Using Isabelle/HOL to verify first-order relativity theory", Journal of Automated Reasoning 52(4), pp. 361-378.

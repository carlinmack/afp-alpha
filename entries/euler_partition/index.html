Euler's Partition Theorem

Lukas Bulwahn

19 November 2015

Abstract

Euler's Partition Theorem states that the number of partitions with only distinct parts is equal to the number of partitions with only odd parts. The combinatorial proof follows John Harrison's HOL Light formalization. This theorem is the 45th theorem of the Top 100 Theorems list.

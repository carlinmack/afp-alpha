Much Ado About Two

Sascha Böhme

6 November 2007

Abstract

This article is an Isabelle formalisation of a paper with the same title. In a similar way as Knuth's 0-1-principle for sorting algorithms, that paper develops a 0-1-2-principle for parallel prefix computations.
